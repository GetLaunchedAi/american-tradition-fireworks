Restricted Fireworks Disclaimer

Professional and Display Fireworks Requirements

Before we can start an order, hold product, arrange shipping, or release pickup for restricted fireworks, we need to review the required documentation.

1.4g Articles Pyrotechnic Fireworks

Requires either a PGI or certified training-course certificate, or a Federal Explosives License to purchase.

These products do not require federal storage compliance for the buyer.

1.3g Display Fireworks

Requires a Federal Explosives License to purchase.

Federal storage requirements apply when these products are stored beyond one day.

  • Send your documentation before we can process the order.
  • If you do not have the required paperwork yet, contact our sales team and we can help point you in the right direction.
  • Read the full requirements and shipping policy for freight, claims, and documentation details.
