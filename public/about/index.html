Common Questions

Frequently asked.

  1. 01

    What types of fireworks do you sell?

    Consumer fireworks, restricted 1.4g products, 1.3g display fireworks, and misc support items such as fuse, igniters, and firing systems.

  2. 02

    What is required for restricted fireworks orders?

    1.4g articles pyrotechnic products need PGI or certified training, or a Federal Explosives License, and they do not require federal storage compliance for the buyer. 1.3g display fireworks require a Federal Explosives License and federal storage compliance beyond one day. We review documentation before order, hold, ship, or pickup. Read full requirements →

  3. 03

    Where are your stores located?

    Our primary store is on G Avenue / 38th Street between Kalamazoo and Battle Creek. We also operate a Springfield store near Dickman Road (M-96) at Military and Admiral Ave.

  4. 04

    How does shipping work?

    We only ship within the continental United States — no Alaska, Hawaii, Canada, U.S. territories, or other countries. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout, freight is FOB origin, and ownership transfers once the order is loaded on the carrier’s truck. Extra freight services at delivery are the customer’s responsibility.

  5. 05

    What should I do if freight is damaged?

    Inspect the shipment on arrival, note any damage or missing items on the driver’s bill of lading, and email photos, your order number, and a short description within 48 hours to support@captainboom.com.
