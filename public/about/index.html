About American Tradition LLC

Since 1984, American Tradition LLC has proudly served Michigan as a veteran-owned and operated business specializing in premium fireworks. As a direct importer, we bring high-quality Consumer, Pro Series, and Display Fireworks to both retail and wholesale customers.

We partner with trusted names like Winda, Panda, and Wizard Fireworks — plus other top manufacturers — to ensure every customer receives safe, reliable, and spectacular products. With two Michigan locations, we’ve built a reputation for excellence, trust, and a commitment to celebrating traditions in style.

Our mission is simple: deliver fireworks that light up your celebrations while providing unmatched value and service. For over four decades, we’ve stood by our community as both neighbors and partners in making every occasion brighter.
