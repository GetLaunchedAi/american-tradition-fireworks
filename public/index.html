About Us

About American Tradition LLC

Founded in 1984, American Tradition LLC is a veteran-owned, Michigan- based fireworks company with two locations. As a direct importer, we provide retail and wholesale fireworks, giving customers access to high-quality products at competitive prices.

We proudly carry top manufacturers like Winda, Panda, and Wizard Fireworks, along with other trusted brands, ensuring every show delivers safety, excitement, and lasting memories. Whether you're stocking up for resale or planning your biggest event yet, our team is here to help you celebrate in style.

Our primary store is on G Avenue / 38th Street between Kalamazoo and Battle Creek, and our Springfield store is near Dickman Road (M-96) at Military and Admiral Ave.

Restricted 1.4g and 1.3g orders require verified paperwork before the sale moves forward, and we can help you understand what is needed before you buy.

Our Products

Wholesale, Retail & Restricted Orders

American Tradition LLC offers a wide range of fireworks, from consumer favorites to restricted 1.4g products, display items, and misc support gear. Every item is carefully sourced to ensure top quality and maximum impact.

With decades of experience, we understand what makes a show truly spectacular. That's why we support both retail customers and wholesale buyers with products that meet their needs, budget, and vision.

Whether you're celebrating a holiday, planning a wedding, hosting a festival, or stocking your own store, American Tradition is your trusted partner for fireworks that deliver.

Need igniters, fuse, firing systems, or other misc items? Those ship alongside the rest of the lineup so buyers can get everything they need in one place.

Shipping is limited to the continental U.S., and restricted orders are released only after the required documentation is verified.
