Here To Help

Get In Touch

Use this form for retail or wholesale orders, restricted-fireworks paperwork, shipping questions, and freight claims. If you are asking about 1.4g or 1.3g products, include the category, the destination, and whether you already have the required training or license.

Shipping is limited to the continental United States, so we do not ship to Alaska, Hawaii, Canada, U.S. territories, or other countries. Freight is FOB origin, and extra freight services at delivery are the customer's responsibility. Rates are confirmed during checkout, and freight issues need to be documented quickly after delivery. The more detail you include up front, the faster we can route your request.

Before you submit

  • 1.4g articles pyrotechnic products need PGI or certified training, or a Federal Explosives License.
  • 1.3g display fireworks need a Federal Explosives License and federal storage compliance beyond one day.
  • Freight claims need photos, order details, and written notice within 48 hours.
