The Operator

Direct importer.
Two stores. Cleared paperwork.

As a direct importer, we bring consumer fireworks, restricted 1.4g articles, 1.3g display ordnance, and misc support gear under one roof — for retail walk-ins and licensed buyers alike.

Started in 1984 as a family operation and never sold up the chain. We learned the trade by buying direct, filing the paperwork ourselves, and standing behind every shipment. Forty years on, that’s still how the place runs.

¶ The paperwork is the product. FEL holders and PGI-certified buyers come to us because we keep our side of the file straight.

Common Questions

Frequently asked.

  1. 01

    What types of fireworks do you sell?

    Consumer fireworks, restricted 1.4g products, 1.3g display fireworks, and misc support items such as fuse, igniters, and firing systems.

  2. 02

    What is required for restricted fireworks orders?

    Two paths, depending on classification:

    • 1.4g restricted articles — PGI or certified training, or a Federal Explosives License (FEL). No federal storage compliance required for the buyer.
    • 1.3g display fireworks — FEL required, plus federal storage compliance for any product held more than one day.
    • Every order — we review documentation before order, hold, ship, or pickup. No documentation, no movement.

    Read full requirements →

  3. 03

    How does shipping work?

    Continental US only — no Alaska, Hawaii, Canada, U.S. territories, or other countries. Rates calculated at checkout. Freight is FOB origin: ownership transfers once the order is loaded on the carrier’s truck. Extra freight services at delivery (lift-gate, residential, appointment) are the customer’s responsibility.

  4. 04

    What should I do if freight is damaged?

    Inspect the shipment on arrival. Note any damage or missing items on the driver’s bill of lading. Email photos, your order number, and a short description within 48 hours to support@captainboom.com.
