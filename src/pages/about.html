About American Tradition LLC

Since 1984, American Tradition LLC has served Michigan as a veteran- owned and operated fireworks company. As a direct importer, we bring consumer fireworks, restricted 1.4g products, display items, and misc support gear to both retail and wholesale customers.

We partner with trusted names like Winda, Panda, and Wizard Fireworks, along with other quality manufacturers, so buyers get dependable products backed by a team that understands what makes a show work. If you are planning a backyard celebration, stocking a store, or building a larger display, we can help you find the right mix of products and the right paperwork.

Our primary store is on G Avenue / 38th Street between Kalamazoo and Battle Creek, and our Springfield store is near Dickman Road (M-96) at Military and Admiral Ave.

Restricted 1.4g articles pyrotechnic fireworks require either PGI or certified training, or a Federal Explosives License. 1.3g display fireworks require a Federal Explosives License and are subject to federal storage requirements beyond one day.

Read the full requirements and shipping policy for documentation rules, freight terms, and freight-claim steps.
