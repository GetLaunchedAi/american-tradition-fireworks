About Us

About American Tradition LLC

Founded in 1984, American Tradition LLC is a veteran-owned, Michigan- based fireworks company with two locations. As a direct importer, we provide retail and wholesale fireworks, giving customers access to high-quality products at competitive prices.

We proudly carry top manufacturers like Winda, Panda, and Wizard Fireworks, along with other trusted brands, ensuring every show delivers safety, excitement, and lasting memories. Whether you're stocking up for resale or planning your biggest event yet, our team is here to help you celebrate in style.

Our primary store is on G Avenue / 38th Street between Kalamazoo and Battle Creek, and our Springfield store is near Dickman Road (M-96) at Military and Admiral Ave.
